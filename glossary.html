ADME
Absorption, Distribution, Metabolism, and Excretion — describes how a drug moves through the body.
Ex vivo
Experiments conducted on tissues or organs outside the living organism, but in controlled conditions.
503A / 503B
US local pharmacy regulations: 503A refers to traditional compounding pharmacies, 503B refers to outsourcing facilities.
